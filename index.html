About

From materials science to building the future of work

My journey started in a research lab, publishing papers on combustion synthesis and materials science. That rigorous, first-principles thinking is what I bring to everything I build.

I transitioned into software engineering, working at companies like Connexity and eLocal, before the pull of entrepreneurship became impossible to ignore. I've since co-founded multiple ventures across different industries.

Today, as CEO of Elixion, I'm solving the problem I kept seeing everywhere: AI tools that work in isolation, disconnected from real engineering workflows. Elixion is where human engineers and AI agents work side by side, with full visibility, shared context, and unified goals.
