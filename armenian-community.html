A Proud Armenian-American

Zhirayr Gumruyan was born in Armenia and served his country for two years in the Armenian Armed Forces as a Senior Surgeon before immigrating to the United States. He carried with him the values, resilience, and determination that define the Armenian spirit. His journey from military service in Armenia to building a life in America is a story shared by thousands of families in District 40.

Deeply connected to the Armenian-American community, Zhirayr understands the unique concerns and values that our community holds dear. From preserving our cultural heritage to supporting our churches, schools, and small businesses, he has lived the Armenian-American experience firsthand.

As a candidate for State Assembly, Zhirayr is committed to representing Armenian-American voices in Sacramento. He understands that our community needs a leader who does not just speak about our issues but has lived them — someone who knows what it means to build a life in a new country while holding onto the traditions and values of our homeland.

Honoring Our History

Zhirayr is committed to ensuring that the Armenian Genocide is fully recognized and its memory preserved. As an Armenian-American serving in the California State Assembly, he will be a voice for truth, justice, and remembrance.

The Armenian Genocide of 1915 must never be forgotten, and its recognition is not just a matter of history — it is a matter of human rights. Every generation has a responsibility to remember the 1.5 million Armenians who perished, to honor their memory, and to stand against genocide and ethnic cleansing wherever they occur in the world.

In Sacramento, Zhirayr will fight to strengthen Armenian Genocide education in California schools, support commemorative efforts, and ensure that our state continues to lead the nation in recognizing historical truth.

Issues That Matter to Our Community

Preserving Armenian Culture

Supporting Armenian schools, churches, and cultural institutions that strengthen our community. Our heritage is our strength, and it must be preserved for future generations.

Small Business Support

Many Armenian families own small businesses. Fighting excessive regulation and taxation that threatens their livelihoods. Our entrepreneurs deserve a government that works with them, not against them.

Family Values

Protecting the family-centered values that are at the heart of Armenian culture. Strong families build strong communities, and our policies should reflect and support that truth.

Community Safety

Ensuring Armenian neighborhoods and businesses are safe and protected. Every family deserves to feel secure in their home and community, and every business owner deserves to operate without fear.

Our Community Partners

Zhirayr is committed to working with the organizations that serve and strengthen our Armenian community.

Armenian National Committee of America (ANCA)

Advancing the interests of the Armenian-American community through advocacy, education, and civic engagement.

Armenian Assembly of America

Promoting public understanding and awareness of Armenian issues through research, advocacy, and community outreach.

Armenian General Benevolent Union (AGBU)

The world's largest Armenian nonprofit organization, dedicated to uplifting Armenians through education, culture, and humanitarian programs.

Armenian Churches & Community Centers

The churches and community centers across District 40 that serve as the heart and soul of Armenian life, bringing families together and preserving our traditions.
